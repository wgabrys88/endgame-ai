The Vision

A single cheap LLM is too stupid to reliably accomplish complex multi-step tasks.

But five of them, wired together with deterministic mathematics from recent research papers, become something qualitatively different — a colony organism that:

  • Decomposes any goal into expertise-matched subtasks
  • Routes exclusively to the best-fit worker (MoE gate)
  • Executes on the real desktop (files, browser, keyboard, mouse)
  • Verifies outcomes via screen observation
  • Breeds better workers — the fittest survive
  • Evolves its own plugins under failure pressure
  • Runs indefinitely toward any long-term goal
