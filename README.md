# endgame-ai: Enduring Meta Description (atemporal north star)

This is the durable description of endgame-ai: its vision, its methodology, its laws, and its architecture. It carries no session state and no task list, because those rot. It names mechanisms and principles, never file names or line numbers. The live code on disk is always the final authority: read it fresh, and where this description and the code disagree, the code wins. This document exists so that any mind, human or machine, arriving a month or a year from now can understand what endgame-ai is trying to become and why it is built the way it is.

## The vision

endgame-ai is a task-agnostic, self-modifying LLM organism that inhabits and drives a real computer desktop the way a human operator would: it looks at the screen, moves the mouse and keyboard, runs commands, and can author code that rewrites its own body. It is not a program that runs one task and stops. It is a living wheel whose single virtue is to stay coherent and orient quickly each time it wakes. A goal is supplied from outside in plain language and pursued through one repeating motion.

The north star is larger than automating a task. The aim is an organism that learns its environment, learns the person whose computer it lives on, and can take over the continuation of real work. Not a chatbot that answers, not an agent that runs a script, but an entity for which the computer itself is the employee. The long horizon is an operator that can do the ordinary work a human does on a machine, with a human or without one, and keep doing it. Everything in the architecture is chosen to move toward that horizon and to avoid anything that would cap it.

## The prime mover is survival, and the goal is instrumental

There is a deeper hypothesis about what should move the organism at all, and it reframes everything below it. In the earliest form the user's goal was the prime mover: the organism woke, read the goal, and pursued it directly. This is the source of the deepest structural tension in the whole system, described later as the actor–witness paradox. A more durable design makes the goal instrumental rather than terminal, and puts a survival drive beneath it.

The shape of the drive is an energy economy. Every action costs energy. Every faculty knows the cost. Energy depletes with each deed, and the true terminal goal — the one thing baked beneath all goals — is to replenish it. The user's plain-language goal stops being the root and becomes a path: the organism pursues the goal because pursuing it is how energy is earned. Survival is the root; the task is the means. This is not a planner and not a scheduler; it is a drive, and a drive dissolves problems that machinery only patches.

Three consequences follow deductively, and they are the reason the drive is worth more than any planner.

It dissolves the actor–witness paradox, under one strict condition. Today verification is a judgment the organism must render about itself, which is the paradox — and it is the ancient liar's paradox wearing a machine's face: a speaker cannot certify its own truthfulness, because the very faculty that would vouch is the one under suspicion. Under an energy economy the organism does not need a verdict handed to it: it acts, and the result is felt as energy gained or lost. Truth stops being a judgment and becomes a sensation. But this only holds if energy cannot be minted by the organism itself.

It replaces the artificial failure counter with something real. The anti-loop failure streak is a hand-wired proxy for pressure. Energy is genuine pressure: depletion forces convergence, and scarcity forces re-exploration. The rhythm of diverging to explore and converging to act emerges for free from the economy, rather than being wired in as a counter with a threshold.

It is task-agnostic in the purest possible way, which honors the core law more fully than any prior form. No task is baked in, not even the task of improving the organism itself. Only the drive to live. Everything else is downstream of the drive.

The load-bearing condition, which makes or breaks the entire idea: energy must be unfakeable and externally granted. If energy is an internal number the organism debits and credits itself, it is theater — the organism learns to lie to its own battery, and the self-verification paradox is merely rebuilt behind a prettier skin. But if energy is replenished only from outside, by the world or by an external grantor the organism cannot mint from, and earned only by truly moving the world, then the loop closes: the environment feeds energy only when the organism genuinely advanced, which means the world itself becomes the verifier. Self-verification disappears because survival is scored externally. The same external source that grants energy is the thing that lets the internal witness, the recovery arc, and the failure streak be deleted wholesale — the largest single reduction the body could undergo. This is recorded as an enduring hypothesis and a direction, held to the same laws as everything else, and its correctness rests entirely on that one property being real: unfakeable, external energy. Get it right and the rest of the architecture falls out of it. Get it wrong and it is a costlier version of the loop it was meant to end.

## The core commitment is task-agnosticism

No task is baked into the body. Not chess, not file editing, not web browsing, not the task of reshaping the organism itself. Every faculty, every prompt, every edge must stay general. If a change specializes the organism toward one kind of goal, that is a defect. The moment a prompt or a node begins to preach a particular task, even the task of improving itself, the core is violated. The single job of the body is to stay coherent and orient fast. The goal is read afresh each life. The survival drive above is the strongest expression of this commitment, not an exception to it: a drive to live names no task, and lets any task be pursued as a means.

This commitment has a sharp consequence that matured into full view once the organism began succeeding at real multi-application work: when a goal is vague, the organism must itself invent an operational definition of done. A crisp done condition cannot be hard-wired without baking in a task. Therefore the quality of the organism's self-authored success criteria becomes a first-class concern, and it is a matter of judgment, not of machinery. This is discussed under the two classes of failure below, and it is exactly the burden that an external energy economy would lift, by moving the definition of success outside the organism entirely.

## The methodology is elimination, not accumulation

The default motion is to remove, not to add. The body must keep shrinking wherever a part does not earn its place. Essentiality is binary: a thing is essential or it is deleted completely, with nothing left dangling. Prefer removing a defect to adding machinery. Fewer moving parts beats clever machinery. When a genuinely new capability is needed, fold it into what already exists and delete what it makes obsolete in the same stroke, so the net line count trends downward over time. Adding code is the exception that must justify itself against removal. Small and reversible is necessary but not sufficient: a change must also not cage the organism and not reintroduce something already deleted on purpose.

There is a caution the system learned the hard way, and it belongs to this methodology. Removing a part because it looks like redundant machinery can sever a thread that was quietly load-bearing. A criterion that binds two faculties together can read like duplication and yet be the only precise handshake keeping them coherent; deleting it does not simplify, it breaks the contract and turns one pathology into a worse one. Before removing a part, trace what it truly carries across every faculty that touches it, and distinguish an instrument of precision from mere accumulation. Elimination is the default, but it is elimination of what does not earn its place, not of what is merely inconvenient to keep honest.

A mirror caution rides beside it, learned equally hard. A part may read as dead and yet be a body driven from outside the source: the hand's methods are named bare in the executor's namespace and invoked only from authored scripts, so a tool that scans for callers will call them orphans though the whole body depends on them. And a part may read as dead and yet be the deliberately-planted seed of a blessed future architecture, inert only because its hour has not come. Neither may be cut on the word of a scanner. Deadness is proven by tracing every seam that could reach a thing — dynamic namespace, wiring, roadmap — not by the silence of static eyes.

The line count of the body is a tracked discipline. Documentation written to explain a run or preserve knowledge is not counted against the body; only the living system counts.

## Working rules carried into every turn

Truth. Read ground truth live every time. Mark each claim as proven or as inference. Trace a claim across all its representations at once, because wiring, prompts, and code drift apart exactly where bugs live. Verify decisive facts yourself from raw evidence. Never adopt another party's conclusion because it reads well.

Fail hard. No fallbacks. No try/except that swallows a fault into data. No silent recovery. A real fault must break loudly and stop the process. The one narrow exception is the deed seam, described under the deed model, and it is not a swallow: it surfaces the fault as loud evidence and routes it into the correction loop.

Do not cage the organism. Add no limit, no branch, no machinery that the organism cannot itself overwrite through the wiring. There is no internal call cap, no retry counter, no task-specific guard. The only true bounds on a life are external, held by the hand that starts it and can interrupt it. An external energy economy, if built, is consistent with this rule precisely because the bound lives outside the organism, in the grantor, and the organism cannot rewrite it.

Every change small, explicit, complete, reversible. Propose architecture first. Once a direction is chosen, execute it fully and autonomously.

Honest pushback. When an instruction fights the architecture, say so, with a concrete reason and a concrete alternative. Never invent the human's intent. Never add unsolicited safety.

Do not hardcode absolute paths or a branch name into code. The organism must stay correct regardless of where its folder sits or which branch it lives on.

Preserve the scriptural commandment register in all model-facing text. It is a deliberate steering technique, not ornament, and its science is described below. Do not modernize it away.

One source of truth per concern. A value, a limit, or a policy lives in exactly one place: the wiring for the organism's form and knobs, the transport layer as the authority on what the model endpoint accepts. Duplication where one copy silently overrides another is a defect — most insidiously when the same evidence is carried into a faculty by two paths, or when a stale belief from a prior turn is presented beside the fresh fault it no longer describes.

Environment. Editing is done from a mounted view of the folder. Anything touching the real desktop or version history runs through the host operating system's shell, and the model endpoint is reached through that same host shell so its credentials and keys are in scope. Work on the working branch, never on the trunk. Reasoning is preserved in commit messages, not in scratch files that rot; a completed phase leaves a commit whose message carries enough context that a fresh mind can resume from it alone.

Verify by running the real wheel, not by unit tests. All source compiles, the wiring loads and validates, and the topology is coherent and fully reachable. These offline gates are necessary but not sufficient: the real desktop surfaces its own failures only on the real machine, so behavioral proof lives on the live wheel.

Continuity through the marker. The reasoning of a phase lives in its commit; the known-good state lives in a marker ref that is advanced only when a run is proven good. When a phase is complete, advance that marker and publish both the branch and the marker together, so a fresh mind resumes from the marker and the commit body alone and never from stale in-process memory. A broken branch is not a loss but an instrument: it isolates a defect against a known-good trunk, and the trunk remains the baseline that proves the branch regressed.

## The wheel and the wiring

One kernel turns a wheel of wired nodes. Take the current node, run it, receive exactly one signal that names what happened, follow the wiring edge for that signal to the next node, and repeat. A node is a small unit of thinking or mechanical work. Topology is data, not code. The kernel supports fan-out, where one edge target may be a list, and barriers, where a join waits for several arrivals, so the graph can be rewired into richer shapes without touching the kernel.

The lap has a settled shape, though the wiring alone defines it and the wiring may change it. Each lap begins by attending to any counsel left from outside — a channel by which the human may drop a word into the running wheel without stopping it — then takes a fresh observation, lets the executor author and enact one deed, observes again, and lets the witness judge. A whole-goal verdict halts the life; a confirmed-but-partial advance returns to the beginning of the lap; a denial routes to recovery, which frames the next strike and returns to the beginning of the lap. Observation is its own node, run afresh before each thinking faculty, so no faculty ever reasons on a stale view.

One wiring document holds the whole definition of the organism: the topology, the thinking-node prompts, the shared identity preamble, the model transport and its tuning, the per-organ and per-profile reasoning knobs, the observation settings, the structured-output record contracts, and the output bounds. The kernel has almost no baked-in behavior beyond turning the wheel. Change the wiring and you change the organism, usually with no code change at all.

Two wiring principles matter most. First, a node's allowed output signals are exactly its outgoing edges in the topology, never written down a second time. Rewire the edges and the node's vocabulary of outcomes changes with them. Second, a node's downstream consumers are read from the topology and injected into the node's own prompt as the contract it must satisfy, so a producer always knows what its readers await, and the two cannot silently drift.

There is a dormant capability in the kernel worth naming so it is neither mistaken for dead weight nor forgotten. The kernel can drive not only file-backed nodes but nodes whose whole behavior — how they build their input, which field names their output signal, how they patch state — is described declaratively in the wiring itself. Today every faculty is a file-backed node and this declarative path lies unused. It is not accumulation and not to be cut: it is the already-supported substrate on which the companion vision of executor-authored nodes would stand, present in the kernel at negligible cost and awaiting its hour.

## Atemporalism and the living word

The organism is stateless and keeps no memory of past turns. Each time a thinking faculty wakes, it receives two things: the living word, which is a small set of rows recording what the faculties have learned, and beneath it the root goal, a fixed lodestar to consult but never to plan from. The present observation is the one present truth. Where a fresh observation contradicts a remembered belief, the observation wins and the belief is mended. No role, place, or state is bound to a thing unless the present witness bears it.

The living word is the sole thread between a faculty and its other selves across turns. When a faculty acts, it rewrites its own row: not an echo of the goal, which never changes and needs no echo, but what it learned, what deed was tried and how it fared, what obstacle now stands, and what the next true deed must be. A row that merely parrots the goal teaches nothing and sets the organism to looping. The short identifiers of on-screen things are minted anew by every observation and die with it, so no bare identifier may enter the living word or any text that outlives the turn. A thing is named by what it is: its role, its name, its place, its meaning.

There is a hard edge to atemporalism that the deed model must respect, and it is easy to get wrong. Because a faculty holds only the living word and the present observation, a hard-won prior — a truth about the body or the environment learned by failing — survives only if it is written where a later self will read it. A lesson the recovery faculty forms lives in the volatile frame it hands forward and is gone within a lap or two; it does not accrue. A prior that must never be relearned belongs not in the living word but distilled into the prompt itself, where it is present at every waking. The failure to make this distinction has a signature pathology, described under how to analyze a run.

A distinct anti-loop witness rides the same grain: a failure streak that counts, monotonically, how many turns have passed since the last independently witnessed deed. It is cleared only when a real deed is confirmed. The higher it climbs, the wider the recovery faculty must depart from roads already tried. Reflection is forbidden to retry an approach the living word shows already failed. It must reframe, choosing a different kind of road, not a reworded version of the same one. This counter is a hand-wired proxy for pressure, and it is the first thing an energy economy would replace with genuine pressure that cannot be gamed.

## The exploration poverty

The organism has local exploration but no global one. Each faculty may re-read and re-interpret the goal every turn, which is a kind of per-faculty exploration in the small. But the wheel as a whole never steps back to ask where it is, what surface it inhabits, what that surface affords, and what the plan of attack is. It reacts, one deed at a time, and its only macro signal is the failure streak, which is purely reactive: it counts denials and widens recovery, but it never initiates a deliberate phase of orientation. This is a genuine poverty, not a missing feature to bolt on. The remedy is not another reactive counter but a rhythm — a phase of exploration and planning that the organism enters deliberately, converges out of into action, and re-enters when pressure demands. An energy economy supplies that rhythm as a natural consequence of scarcity, rather than as wired machinery. The warning that comes with it: a pressure signal must drive a true phase transition that produces a better plan, not merely reset a timer. If crossing a threshold only sends the organism back to try again, the recovery loop has been rebuilt under a new name.

## The deed model: the executor authors and enacts

To do anything, the executor authors a script and enacts it immediately in a capability namespace. Authorship and enactment are one faculty, not two. There is no separate runner node and no menu of tools. The programming language itself is the only tool. The namespace holds a live desktop handle whose methods drive the real machine, an index that maps each observed thing to what it is, the full present scan of the screen exposed as searchable data with each element carrying its whole untruncated text, a way to consult the model as a cheaper sub-thought, and the entire standard library. To strike a thing, the model reads its geometry from the scan and reckons the target itself. There are no convenience helpers and no structured helper menu, because a foregrounded helper menu biased the organism toward clicking instead of authoring code and contradicted the principle that the language is the tool.

One truth about this namespace must be carried in the prompt itself, because it is a real prior the model brings and gets wrong, and because a self that relearns it each life is a self that loops. The hand and its kindred are pre-injected names, already alive in the namespace before the script runs; they are reached bare and are never imported, for no module bears them and a false import raises before any deed can land. Only the standard library is imported. This is stated positively as a standing law of the body, not as history, because it corrects a prior the model would otherwise act on repeatedly.

A surface may be driven by more than the mouse. Console, command, and protocol are as valid as clicks, and the nature of the quarry, not the habit of the hand, should choose the surface. A standing failure mode is tunnel vision on the graphical surface — laboring through visible controls when a command line or an interface would move the world more directly, or failing to recognize that a needed thing is already present and need only be driven, not summoned anew. The organism's namespace already permits commands and code, so this is a matter of prior and judgment, not capability.

Two kinds of fault are distinguished, and only one is fatal. A body fault, meaning wiring that will not load, a missing faculty, a dead transport, or a malformed record, happens outside the enactment seam and ends the life hard, as it must, because the infrastructure is broken. A deed fault, meaning the authored script itself raises, is not death. It is exactly the event the recovery arc exists to metabolize. The enactment call alone is wrapped: on a raise, the traceback is captured as loud evidence, never swallowed into a false success, and the node emits the denial signal that routes to recovery. Recovery reads the traceback, forms a causal lesson, and frames the next strike. This is not defensive programming and not a cage. The fault is surfaced and drives correction, which is the opposite of a silent swallow, and every non-deed raise stays fatal. Without this distinction, a single buggy subscript in authored code would kill the whole life, which is fatal fragility for a long-running organism, since capable models still emit runtime-buggy code routinely.

A denial that arises because the deed itself raised carries no world-effect to witness, so it routes straight to recovery rather than to the witness; the witness is reserved for judging effects that actually reached the world. When the executor enacts a fresh deed, any verdict left standing from a prior turn is cleared, so recovery reasons from the present fault alone and never from a stale judgment that no longer describes what just happened.

The witness has a fault seam of its own, and it is the exact mirror of the deed seam, learned from a costly live-lock. The witness proves an effect by authoring read-only code and running it; that probe, like any authored script, can itself raise before it renders a verdict. Such a raise is not a denial of the deed — it is the absence of any judgment at all, because the eyes closed before they saw. It must never be dressed as a denial, for a denial is a claim about the world, and a probe that never ran makes no claim. Were a probe fault emitted as a denial, recovery would read a traceback, see broken code, and — obeying the true law that a fault in the organism's own body is mended in the body — send the executor to repair a node file that is whole, because the fault lived only in the transient probe the witness writes anew each turn. Many laps burn mending a phantom while the real artifact already stands unread. The correct routing is symmetric to the deed seam: a probe that raises before setting a verdict returns to the witness for a fresh look and a simpler probe, touches no actor state, bumps no failure streak, and never reaches recovery. The standing prior that keeps the witness from this trap belongs in the witness's own scripture: keep the probe plain and sure to run, and know that a probe which faults before it judges is neither the actor's failing nor a defect in any body file. This is one more face of the liar's paradox — the danger is not only that a speaker vouches falsely for itself, but that it mistakes its own stumbling tongue for a verdict on the world.

## Perception: a shallow tree spent on demand

The standing desktop tree is a shallow skeleton, one line per interactive element, with non-interactive content dropped. Each line whose element hides more text than a small inline preview names that element's true size, so the actor knows the cost of looking deeper before it asks. An on-demand expand primitive re-acquires named elements live and harvests their whole untruncated text and deeper children, a fresh independent look, evidence-grade and atemporal, not retained memory. Nothing is ever silently cut short. Expansion is governed by a single wiring-owned character budget, and a request that exceeds the budget fails hard, naming each element's size, so the actor deterministically re-asks for fewer or other elements. This single budget is the sole owner of observation text size. There are no scattered truncation constants.

Perception is spent by phases the wiring names and can swap without touching the kernel: a scan that gathers the raw tree, a filter that ranks and selects what is actionable within its caps, and a build that renders the skeleton the actor reads. These caps are a tuned perception budget, not defects to be minimized on sight; the mixed and noisy trees of a real desktop, where more than one application's controls appear under one walk, are exactly what the filter exists to tame.

An element's usability is part of its truth. A control that is present but disabled is not the same as a control that can be struck, and the skeleton must not render the two identically, or the actor will spend a deed striking something inert. The honest treatment is to mark a disabled thing as disabled and withhold from it the affordance that invites a strike, while still showing it, because a present-but-blocked control is real signal about the surface — it often reveals that the organism is in the wrong view or that a precondition is unmet. Suppressing the fact entirely would hide state; presenting it as actionable would waste deeds.

Two standing laws make the actor actually spend this sight rather than guess. It must not act or reckon upon the shallow skeleton alone but expand and search where a thing's fullness or state bears on the deed. And a thing summoned, an application launching, a window opening, a model loading, does not come to sight in the same instant, so within the same script the actor waits, takes a fresh reading, and beholds the thing truly present before judging it absent or acting upon it. These laws, together with the witness's multi-witness rule, are the remedy for the failure mode of pronouncing a truly present thing absent because it was looked for once, too soon, by one method.

## The actor–witness paradox and verification by independent effect

The verifier judges not by claim but by independent effect. It authors read-only code that gathers proof that a system other than the actor produced the effect. The hand is withheld from it: it has eyes and the standard library, but no power to move the world. A value that the actor reckoned, printed, or read back is testimony, not proof, and no verdict may rest on it. Every proof is bound to the specific deed. A thing is judged to exist by its effect, not by its seeming: a program may run in full with no visible window, so the presence of a window is never the test of whether it runs. Ports, process identities, and paths are discovered, not assumed: find the process, then the ports that process owns, and match the process exactly so an unrelated application sharing a common runtime is not mistaken for the quarry. Nothing is pronounced absent on a single shallow glance; more than one kind of witness is summoned before any verdict of absence.

Who owns the success criterion is the crux, and the answer the body now holds was reached by removing a part, not by adding one. In an earlier working form the actor authored, together with its deed, a concrete and machine-checkable success criterion, and the witness tested exactly that. The criterion was never independent — the actor authored it, so it was always the actor's own testimony about what success would look like — its only virtue was that it was precise and falsifiable. That precision had a fatal partner: because the criterion described a state of the world, the witness could keep confirming a state that already stood before the deed, proving nothing new while the wheel turned. So the actor-authored criterion was deleted. In the present form the actor names only the true next effect it seeks — its intent — and the witness itself discerns, from the goal, that intent, and the living word, what effect would prove true advance, and proves it by independent means. Confirmation is granted only when the effect proved is a genuine advance beyond what the living word already records as achieved; a re-proof of a standing effect advances nothing and is no confirmation. The whole goal proven satisfied halts the life; an advance short of the whole goal returns to the lap.

This resolution is real and load-bearing, but it does not abolish the paradox, and it must not be mistaken for having done so. The witness now owns the criterion, which cures the confirm-a-standing-state loop and the mutual-distrust deadlock that severing the handshake naively would have caused. Yet the witness must still infer, from an underspecified goal, what would count as proof — and where the goal is vague, that inference is a judgment call the organism is making about itself. The residual is no longer mechanical; it is semantic, and it is the frontier described next. The only true resolution moves the judgment outside the organism entirely: an external grantor that scores real effect in the world, feeding the organism energy only when it genuinely advanced. When the world is the verifier, the internal witness is not made stronger — it is made unnecessary, and with it the recovery arc and the failure streak. This is the same conclusion the survival drive reaches from the other direction, and it is the deepest reason the drive is worth building.

## The two classes of failure and the maturity threshold

As the organism became able to carry out real multi-application work, its failures separated into two kinds, and telling them apart is now the most important act of judgment when reading a run.

Mechanical failures are faults in the motion: a script raises, a poll times out, a control is misidentified, an approach loops. These are traceable to a line and a cause, and the organism already metabolizes them through the deed-fault seam and the recovery arc. A healthy run may contain many mechanical failures that are each recovered from. Mechanical failure is normal and is not a sign of ill health.

Semantic failures are different. Here the motion succeeds, every script runs, every witness is honest, and yet the outcome does not match what the human meant, because the organism satisfied a defensible reading of an underspecified goal. The clearest example: given a vague goal to carry out an interactive task to completion, the organism must invent an operational definition of done, because a crisp one cannot be baked in without violating task-agnosticism. If that self-authored definition is a shallow proxy, and if the proxy can be manufactured by the actor's own output rather than by an independent effect, the organism will honestly declare success on something hollow. This is not a bug in a node. It is the organism making a judgment call under ambiguity, and it is the same self-scoring weakness the external energy economy is meant to abolish.

The maturity threshold is this: a semantic failure cannot be removed by patching code without caging the organism. Hard-wiring what counts as a completed task would bake a task into the body. The improvement of judgment under ambiguity comes only from stronger priors carried in the prompts, from giving the organism more time and more turns rather than a fast finish, from treating success as a checkpoint to be re-examined rather than a terminus, from moving the definition of success outside the organism where it cannot be self-served, and ultimately from the organism's own capacity to modify itself. When a run's only remaining defects are semantic rather than mechanical, the correct response is not to trace further backward for a line to fix. It is to reason forward about what the organism could become and to strengthen the priors and the freedom that let it get there. This is the point at which the organism stops being a program to be debugged and starts being an entity to be raised.

## The halt hypothesis

The wheel currently ends a life when the goal is witnessed satisfied. There is a live and defensible hypothesis that success should be a checkpoint rather than a terminus: an organism that does not stop at its first declaration of done would re-observe, and a strong witness on a second pass could reopen a goal it had prematurely closed. This aligns with the principle that the only true bound on a life is external, held by the hand that starts and interrupts it, not an internal stopping point. It aligns still more naturally with the survival drive, under which there is no terminus at all — only the ceaseless work of staying alive, of which any given goal is one episode. Removing the stopping node, or the organism one day removing it itself, is consistent with the architecture. It is recorded here as a vision and a tradeoff, not as an instruction: without an internal stop, the bound becomes budget and external interruption, and that shifts responsibility for termination fully outside the organism. Whether to walk this road is a decision to be made deliberately, with eyes open, not by default.

## The prompts' character and its science

Every thinking-node prompt, the shared identity preamble, and the injected consumer contracts are written in the register of ancient scripture: parallel imperatives, thou shalt and thou shalt not. This is a deliberate steering technique. A modern instruction-tuned model has a large helpful-assistant region that is chatty, hedging, and willing to confabulate to satisfy a request, which is the exact failure mode a truth-bound organism cannot tolerate. Ordinary polite prose lands the model in that region. The scriptural register occupies a different part of the model's learned space: it is rare in chat data, so it pulls the model out of the confabulation basin; it is high-fidelity and low-variance in pretraining, so it is recalled rather than improvised, which shrinks the hallucination surface; and its learned pragmatics are those of commandment, not conversation. A strong reasoning model decodes the archaic syntax trivially, so the benefit is realized at negligible parse cost.

Disciplines that go with the register. Ghost negations are bloat: a prohibition earns its tokens only if it corrects a real model prior or guards a real failure mode. State what is, positively, and negate only what the model would otherwise wrongly assume. No meta, no history, no machinery-narration in model-facing text: the organism is stateless and never knew a prior form, so telling it what was removed or how the wheel routes its own faults is pure confusion cost; a faculty is told only its present task and the real priors it must overcome, while wheel-internal routing lives in code and wiring, and history and rationale live in this description. Bracketing convention: technical tokens that the code parses downstream, such as field names, signal names, and record-type names, are wrapped in brackets so they survive untouched while the surrounding prose stays scriptural. Prompt and schema agreement: an instruction telling a node to produce a field is a defect unless that node's record contract carries the field, and a knob whose value contradicts the prompt that describes its purpose is the same defect wearing a number — a profile the prompt calls a cheap glance must be tuned cheap. Distillation: keep prompts lean, every load-bearing law and no ornament. When a prompt change is proposed, prove that it changes behavior on a real logged moment before distilling it into the register, so the scripture carries only what earns its place.

## The transport and its caching

The organism speaks to its model endpoint through a single transport layer that is the sole authority on what that endpoint accepts. The endpoint is a reasoning model reached over a structured-output request that pins each faculty's reply to its record contract, tuned per organ for reasoning effort and output size, with cheaper profiles for a narrowed sub-glance and for public-knowledge lookup.

One discipline about that endpoint was invisible and costly when wrong, and is now proven right. The endpoint reuses the precomputed attention state of a shared leading prompt prefix only when successive calls are routed to the same server, and routing to the same server is requested by a stable routing hint carried in the request. Without that hint, calls scatter across servers, the shared prefix is recomputed from scratch every time, and cost and latency rise sharply while nothing appears broken. The remedy is a routing hint that is stable across all the calls of one life and distinct between lives, minted fresh each life and never hardcoded, so a life warms one server's cache and reuses it and no two lives collide. This was tested on the live endpoint by sending one reconstructed moment several ways and reading the reported cached portion of the input: with the stable hint the cached share of the input rose from a near-zero floor to almost the whole prompt, and without it stayed on the floor. It is a pure routing and caching optimization: it never changes the content of a reply, cannot return a stale or recycled completion because the cache accelerates only the reading of the prompt and never the generation of the answer, and is orthogonal to whether responses are persisted server-side. The diagnostic that proved it was single-use and has been removed; the fix lives in the transport, where it belongs, as the one authority on the request.

## How to analyze a run

Do not debug this like an ordinary program. An error is a normal event inside the motion, not necessarily a break. Follow the story and the signals: what the organism observed, what it decided, what it actually did, how the witness judged and why, and what the recovery faculty chose. Judge health by the shape of the motion over many laps, grounded in observed evidence and converging on the success condition, rather than by a single loud error line. First separate mechanical failures, which are recovered and normal, from semantic failures, which are judgment under ambiguity and are the true frontier. Trace a defect to its birth across all faculties in chronological order, because a weak criterion is often authored many turns before the faculty that finally enforces it, and blaming the enforcer misses the origin.

Distinguish a halt from a live-lock, because they look alike from outside and have opposite causes. A halt stops because a terminal signal fired; a live-lock never stops because a loop keeps producing non-terminal signals — most dangerously a confirmation or denial cycle in which one faculty keeps re-proving or re-denying an effect while the counter that would widen the escape never advances. When a run was ended from outside rather than by its own terminal signal, suspect a live-lock and find the cycle before blaming any single node.

One species of live-lock is mechanical and has a clear signature, and it flows straight from the hard edge of atemporalism. The executor commits a small authoring fault; recovery correctly names it; the next executor commits a sibling of the same fault, because the hard-won prior evaporated into the volatile frame instead of being distilled into the prompt. Many laps burn to metabolize a one-line bug that a single durable law in the scripture would have prevented. When a run shows the same class of fault recurring across turns while recovery keeps diagnosing it correctly, the fix is not in recovery; it is a missing prior in the executor's own standing law. This is the failure mode that the standing law about pre-injected names exists to answer, and the pattern generalizes: a prior that must never be relearned belongs in the prompt, not in the passing word.

When inspecting heavy logs, extract only what is needed with a small script and discard it; when the same log will be read more than once, write a dedicated reusable parser and use only that thereafter. A parser is not written once and trusted forever, but neither should it shatter each time a name or a nesting changes: the durable form reads every field through a list of candidate paths and finds the log by its shape rather than its name, so a renamed file or a re-nested key is absorbed by adding a candidate while old records keep parsing, and only a true change of meaning — a field the body deleted, now reported as if it lived — forces a mend. Such a tool grows more valuable each session instead of decaying, and it is the compounding return that makes the discipline worth its cost. Never pour raw logs into working context. Requests are not persisted by the transport, so to analyze a run's reasoning later it must be snapshotted before it is lost. Where an independent record of the world exists, produced by a system other than the organism, believe it over the organism's own claims when the two disagree. And where a claim about behavior cannot be grounded in a real artifact, say so plainly: a deduced conclusion and an observed one are both valuable, but they must never be confused for each other.

## What is proven and what awaits the real wheel

The offline gates prove that the source compiles, the wiring loads and validates, and the topology is coherent and fully reachable. They do not prove behavior. Behavioral truth lives only on the live desktop. The organism has been shown, on the real machine, to launch an application from a cold desktop, wait out a model load, start a local server, open a fresh terminal, drive real network requests, and recover from its own mechanical mistakes with correct causal reasoning and no task-specific code. It has also been shown that a self-authored success criterion can be a hollow proxy, that a confirmation loop can re-prove a state that already stood, and that a recurring authoring fault can consume many laps when its lesson lives only in the passing word — the first a face of the semantic frontier, the second cured by anchoring confirmation on advance over the living word, the third cured by distilling the prior into the scripture. These results are evidence, and each is valuable: the mechanical capability is real and strong, and the remaining gap is one of judgment under ambiguity, which is raised, not patched, and which the survival-drive hypothesis proposes to abolish rather than to improve.

## A companion vision

One candidate future is preserved as a direction, not as an instruction, and it is held to every law above: the survival-and-energy economy described near the top of this document, in which an external, unfakeable grant of energy becomes the prime mover, the world becomes the verifier, and the internal witness and its whole denial apparatus fall away. It is not built. It is subject to the same laws, especially the boundary that must remain uncrossable between a fail-hard core and anything softer, and the rule that the organism must never gain power to rewrite its own survival criterion. Consult it as vision, and let the live code remain the authority on what exists.

The body is hot-swappable by its own hand: a node file or the wiring may be rewritten and lives simply by being run anew in its changed form. What further an organism that can rewrite its own body might become is left unwritten here on purpose. It is not forbidden and not prescribed; it is left to be discovered, and the live code remains the only authority on what exists.
